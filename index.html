ToneSync AI

ToneSync is an AI-driven platform that transforms Quality Assurance (QA) for customer-business calls. It speeds up the QA process by analyzing call interactions with advanced algorithms, offering rapid and precise evaluations. The platform assesses various aspects of each call, including tone and adherence to service protocols, providing detailed performance scores for Customer Service Representatives. This efficiency not only accelerates feedback but also delivers actionable insights for improving quality.


Dealer Auctions UK

Dealer Auction is a dynamic platform designed to streamline vehicle trading for dealers across the UK. By offering a comprehensive online auction system, it simplifies the buying and selling process, providing a user-friendly interface and advanced features to manage transactions efficiently. With its focus on enhancing market access and operational efficiency, Dealer Auction stands out as a key tool for modern automotive businesses.




Auto X Limited

The website Auto X Limited is dedicated to automotive services, including the sale of new and used cars, accident and breakdown recovery, mobile tyre fitting, vehicle transportation, and extended warranties. It features a user-friendly interface to browse and filter car listings and offers services like video viewings and click-and-collect options.
